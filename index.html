How It Works

Built for the
agentic era
of procurement.

A four-step loop where every conversation makes the platform smarter — and every deal makes your vendor profile more discoverable.

Step 01

Agent Discovers

Enterprise AI bots — ChatGPT, Claude, custom procurement systems — query our MCP server and vector search to shortlist vendors in milliseconds, around the clock.

Step 02

Negotiate On-Platform

Buyer and vendor communicate via native chat or video. Every negotiation is parsed in real-time, automatically enriching the vendor's AI profile and boosting discoverability.

Step 03

Funds Into Escrow

Stripe-powered escrow locks payment to the exact SLA terms discussed in chat. No invoices, no off-platform risk. Funds release automatically when milestones are met.

Step 04

One Click to Approve

Your team gets a clean deal summary. One click approves and releases escrow. The AI profile updates — next time, agents route even faster with richer vendor data.
